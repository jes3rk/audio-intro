Welcome to Audio Tools

Audio Tools is an online interactive tutorial for common audio production tools. Entirely on the web with no software needed, this is the perfect place for beginners to learn and interact with common audio tools.

Equlization

Equlization, or EQ, is one of the most commonly used audio processing tools. EQ units range from very simple high and low shelves to large 31 band graphic units. This tutorial slowly builds complexity while teaching you the basics of EQ.
